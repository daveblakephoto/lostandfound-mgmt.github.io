About LOSTANDFOUND Management

Based in Brisbane and Byron Bay, Australia.

LOSTANDFOUND was founded by Dave Blake following years spent working across the fashion industry, both locally and internationally. With a background spanning fashion, photography, and casting, our focus is on thoughtful representation and building genuine connection within the industry.

As a mother agency, we are hands-on in every aspect of our models’ careers, from development through to international placements. We believe that strong relationships, open communication, and mutual trust are essential to creating successful, sustainable careers.

Our roster is small by design. Diverse, intentional, and evolving with care.

  • We value presence over perfection.
  • Story over sameness.
  • People over portfolios.
